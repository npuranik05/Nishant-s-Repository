About Nishant Puranik

I am a dedicated and ambitious Software Engineering student at the University of Western Ontario, with a passion for developing innovative software solutions. My academic journey, focusing on Software Design, Algorithms & Data Structures, and Digital Logic Systems, equips me with a solid foundation in both technical and business skills.

During my internship as a Non-Revenue Vehicle Fleet Analyst at Metrolinx, I led the development of a Python and Excel-based vehicle booking system, which improved internal vehicle scheduling efficiency by 38%. I thrive in leadership roles, managing projects and teams to optimize processes and deliver impactful results.

I have also demonstrated leadership outside the corporate environment as a Green House Advisor, where I developed a fish composting system and led environmental education programs. Additionally, I organized events promoting South Asian culture as a Youth Leader at Kannada Sangha Toronto, enhancing community engagement and increasing membership by 15%.

My technical abilities include proficiency in Java, Python, Javascript, MATLAB, and experience with fleet management software like GeoTab. As a fast learner with a passion for technology and leadership, I am always eager to take on new challenges and contribute to meaningful projects.
